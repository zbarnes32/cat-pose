About Us

Cat Pose was create with a simple idea in mind, what if we practiced yoga with cats. Putting both together creates a fun, unforgettable experience. Partnering with the Idaho Humane Society, all cats and kittens at Cap Pose are adoptable so if you make a special connection with a furry friend, you can make your newfound friend a part of your family.
